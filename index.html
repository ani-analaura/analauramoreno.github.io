AUTHENTIC design

A passion for creating beautiful things

I'm a graphic designer based in Mexico City with 5+ years of experience building visual systems across brand identity, packaging, print, and digital — from campaign key visuals to social assets, web content, and e-commerce materials. I've worked across industries and formats, always leading the creative process end-to-end: briefing, concept, execution, and supplier coordination.What I bring beyond craft is translation — the ability to take a strategic brief and turn it into visuals that actually communicate. I work closely with marketing and product teams, adapt quickly to fast-paced environments, and obsess over the details that make a brand system scalable and consistent across markets.I stay sharp by integrating emerging tools — including AI for 3D rendering — into my workflow where they add real value. Not as a shortcut, but as part of how I keep delivering at a high level.Outside design I box, do ceramics, eat everything, and fiercely protect time with the people I love. I find inspiration in everyday discoveries — that's what keeps my imagination alive. I believe design has the power to connect messages with people, and that's why every project is a chance to make something I'm truly proud of. I'm a firm believer that the brands that really connect are built with love, and I love what I do.
